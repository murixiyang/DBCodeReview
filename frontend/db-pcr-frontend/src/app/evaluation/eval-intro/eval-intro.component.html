Welcome to the DB Code Review Study

Thank you for volunteering to participate in our code-review evaluation.
Your feedback will help us understand how different review conditions affect reviewer behavior and the author experience.

Before Starting

You will need to register an account to participate in this study.
We are using a self-hosted Gitlab so the normal gitlab account you hold won't work. Please register a new one.

Overview of Activities

  1. Optional Authoring Phase (≈15 min)
    If you choose, you may write a short real-world coding snippet. Your code might be reviewed by other participants in the next phase and you’ll receive the comments from the reviewers.
  2. Peer-Review Rounds (2 × 5~10 min)
    You will review two different code snippets, giving comments to them.
  3. Reviewer Survey (≈5 min)
    After both review rounds, you will complete a short questionnaire about your experience. Your identity can be omitted from reports or presentations upon request — just let us know.

What We will Do with Your Responses

We will collect your written code, review comments and survey responses for research purposes.
